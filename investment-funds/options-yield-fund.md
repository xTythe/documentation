# Options Yield Fund

The Options Yield Fund earns yield by harvesting the volatility risk premium through a disciplined options strategy. It is the unbounded version of the options strategy that Managed Savings runs conservatively: where Managed Savings holds that strategy at a small, protective exposure, this fund runs it at maximum viable size to seek return. Its value floats with the strategy's performance, and the investor holds both the return and the risk directly. It is available to verified users.

#### Where the yield comes from

Options consistently price in more volatility than markets go on to deliver. That gap is the volatility risk premium, and it can be harvested by systematically selling options and collecting the premium, which on average exceeds what the market actually moves. The fund runs Synclear's options strategy to capture it.

The strategy is delta-neutral by nature (it hedges directional exposure), so it is not a bet on the market going up or down. Its return and its risk come from volatility itself, not from price direction. When markets are calm relative to what options implied, the strategy earns; when volatility spikes sharply, it can lose.

To contain that, the strategy uses defined-risk structures, positions whose maximum loss is capped in advance rather than left open-ended. No single position can produce an unlimited loss. But a broad, sudden volatility spike can move many positions against the fund at once, and the fund can draw down meaningfully even with each position individually capped.

#### The risk you hold

The Options Yield Fund is a floating-NAV product. Its share price rises when the strategy earns and falls when it loses, and the investor holds that outcome directly. Unlike Managed Savings, there is no reserve behind it, and unlike the Cashflow Credit Fund, there is no first-loss buffer. The fund's market risk is yours by design: it is the reason the fund can pay more than a savings account, and the reason its value can fall.

This is a strategy that earns steadily in most conditions and can lose sharply in rare ones. It suits investors who understand that profile and want exposure to it, not those looking for a stable-value place to hold dollars. For that, the savings accounts are the right choice.

#### **Invest**

Once verified, invest with a supported stablecoin. In return you receive a fund share, an ERC-4626 token representing your stake.

#### **Earn**

The share's value floats with the fund's net asset value. As the strategy earns, the share is worth more; when it loses, the share is worth less. There is nothing to claim; the outcome accrues in the share.

#### Access

The Options Yield Fund requires identity verification (KYC) and is subject to the rules of each user's jurisdiction. Users are responsible for compliance with the laws that apply to them.

#### Insurance

The Options Yield Fund carries smart-contract insurance, provided by Chainproof and Nexus Mutual, covering a technical failure or exploit of its contracts. It carries no reserve and no first-loss buffer. As a floating-NAV strategy, its market risk is held directly by the investor by design, and that market risk is not insured.

#### Risks

The fund carries market and volatility risk: a sharp move can cause a meaningful loss, and its value floats and can fall. Defined-risk structures cap the loss on each individual position but not the loss across the book in a broad volatility spike. The fund also carries the smart-contract and stablecoin risks common to the protocol. These risks are managed through defined-risk structures, directional hedging, and position limits, but they are not eliminated, and they are borne by the investor. Before investing, please read the [Risk Disclosure](../protocol-resources/risk-disclosure.md) in full.
