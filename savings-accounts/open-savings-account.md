# Open Savings Account

Open Savings is the permissionless, conservative foundation of Synclear. Deposit a stablecoin and earn a steady, dollar-denominated return while your funds stay in minimal-risk, dollar-stable positions. It is open to everyone, with no verification, no lockup, and no jurisdictional restriction. Its purpose is simple: protect your capital while it earns.

#### What backs Open Savings

Open Savings holds only conservative, dollar-stable assets, deployed into yield rather than left idle. None of its backing moves with the crypto market.

* **Tokenized treasury bills.** Short-term US government debt, held through tokenized treasury products such as BlackRock's BUIDL and Ondo's USDY. Treasury bills are among the lowest-risk dollar instruments available, and they earn the prevailing short-term rate.
* **On-chain overcollateralized lending.** Stablecoins supplied to established on-chain money markets such as Aave and Morpho, where every loan is backed by more collateral than it lends. This earns lending interest while the overcollateralization protects the supplied capital.
* **Liquid stablecoins as cash.** A portion is held in liquid stablecoins, providing immediate liquidity for withdrawals and ballast for the account.

Each of these is dollar-denominated and holds its value independent of crypto prices. Together they form a conservative portfolio built to protect capital first and earn second.

#### Where the yield comes from

The return on Open Savings is the blended yield of its backing: the treasury-bill rate, the interest earned from on-chain lending, and the yield on stablecoins held as cash. It is a real return generated by conservative, dollar-stable assets, not by taking market risk. Because it is anchored to short-term rates and lending demand, it moves with those rather than with crypto sentiment.

Open Savings is deliberately similar to conservative savings products found elsewhere in the market. What it offers is a dependable, permissionless baseline, not a novel or elevated yield. Users who want more can move to Managed Savings or the investment funds.

Because the Open Savings token follows the ERC-4626 standard and is permissionless, it composes freely across open DeFi. You can hold it, transfer it, or use it elsewhere while it continues to earn.

#### Access

Open Savings is available to everyone. There is no KYC, no verification, and no jurisdictional restriction. It is the one Synclear savings account open to all users.

#### Risks

Open Savings is conservative, but not risk-free. It carries smart-contract risk, since the Synclear contracts and the on-chain venues it uses could contain vulnerabilities, which is why every contract is properly audited before it handles funds. It carries counterparty risk, because the tokenized treasury-bill issuers and lending venues it deploys into are external parties whose failure could affect the backing. And it carries the risk of the third-party stablecoins it holds and deploys, each of which has its own issuer and peg risk. These risks are bounded by conservative design and diversification, but they are not eliminated. Before depositing, please read the [Risk Disclosure](../protocol-resources/risk-disclosure.md) in full.
