# Synclear Dollar (SCD)

{% hint style="info" %}
The Synclear Dollar (SCD) is not the same as a fiat stablecoin such as USDC or USDT. SCD is a synthetic dollar, backed by conservative dollar-stable holdings and a bounded allocation to hedged delta-neutral positions, never by fiat reserves in a bank.

This means the risks of holding SCD are inherently different. Please read the [Risk Disclosure](https://app.gitbook.com/o/0aiW3e41N69QHIAXxWlx/s/DJA9njN2uMlkeZuQfMaD/~/edit/~/changes/16/protocol-resources/risk-disclosure) section before depositing.
{% endhint %}

#### Overview

SCD is a fully-backed, on-chain synthetic dollar, subject to the events described in the Risk Disclosure section. It is permissionless to mint, redeem, hold, save through Open Savings, and trade, and it composes freely throughout DeFi.

SCD does not earn on its own. The simplest way to put it to work is sSCD, its permissionless saved form, which passes the return the backing generates to savers. Verified users have further ways to earn, covered under [Savings Accounts](https://app.gitbook.com/s/DJA9njN2uMlkeZuQfMaD/savings-accounts) and [Strategy Funds](https://app.gitbook.com/s/DJA9njN2uMlkeZuQfMaD/strategy-funds). The specific actions, minting, redeeming, saving, stabilizing, and borrowing, each have their own page.

#### What backs SCD

SCD's backing is held entirely in dollar-stable value, never in fiat reserves in a bank.

Most of it is dollar-stable by construction: liquid stablecoins, short-term instruments such as tokenized treasury bills, and on-chain money-market lending, each worth a dollar and deployed into yield rather than left idle. Their value does not move with the crypto market.

A bounded portion is held in delta-neutral positions (such as the Synclear Blended Options DN, Ethena Crypto Funding DN, and Theo Gold Carry DN). Where the backing is a volatile asset, the protocol holds a short of equal notional against it, so the combined position holds its dollar value regardless of which way the market moves. Volatile backing is never left unhedged, the allocation is kept bounded, and a protocol-owned reserve is sized to absorb its tail.

Synclear is a CeDeFi protocol. The conservative holdings sit in the protocol's own on-chain contracts and in vetted on-chain venues. The delta-neutral hedge legs run on vetted derivatives venues under off-exchange settlement, where backing is custodied by off-exchange providers and only delegated to a venue to margin the hedge, never transferred to it. There is no fiat bank anywhere in the backing.

This is also what lets SCD scale safely. The conservative core can be held without limit, and the protocol never forces a hedge onto a venue that cannot absorb it. When funding is poor or hedging capacity is reached, backing simply stays in the conservative holdings. SCD supply is therefore bounded by the depth of dollar-stable backing across DeFi, not by the open interest of any single venue.

#### Peg stability

SCD holds its peg structurally, from the nature of its backing rather than from a defended reserve. Because the conservative holdings are the majority and the hedged portion is bounded and reserve-covered, each SCD is backed by a dollar of value that can always be redeemed for roughly a dollar. And because redemption returns a dollar, open-market arbitrage continuously pulls the traded price back whenever it drifts.

The full mechanics, the arbitrage path, the instant redemption buffer, and the stress protections, are covered in [Stabilize SCD](https://app.gitbook.com/o/0aiW3e41N69QHIAXxWlx/s/DJA9njN2uMlkeZuQfMaD/~/edit/~/changes/16/introduction/synclear-dollar-scd/stabilize-scd).

#### Where the yield comes from

SCD does not earn on its own. The return generated by the backing is passed to savers through sSCD and the gated savings tier, from several independent sources: interest from on-chain money-market lending, yield on short-term instruments such as tokenized treasury bills, stablecoin-native yield, and the funding captured by the bounded delta-neutral positions. Because these do not all depend on the same market condition, the backing produces yield across bull, neutral, and bear regimes while prioritizing safety and stability of the Synclear Dollar.

The Strategy Funds are separate and ring-fenced, and are not part of SCD's backing. Full detail can be found under [Savings Accounts](https://app.gitbook.com/o/0aiW3e41N69QHIAXxWlx/s/DJA9njN2uMlkeZuQfMaD/~/edit/~/changes/16/savings-accounts).

#### Risks

Holding SCD carries risks that differ from a fiat-backed stablecoin, including but not limited to:

* Smart contract risk
* Market and hedging risk
* Venue and execution risk
* Oracle risk
* Liquidity risk
* Custody and counterparty risk

Synclear mitigates these through a conservative-majority backing, multi-venue diversification, position limits, circuit breakers, oracle redundancy, and a protocol-owned reserve, but they cannot be completely eliminated. Please read the [Risk Disclosure](https://app.gitbook.com/o/0aiW3e41N69QHIAXxWlx/s/DJA9njN2uMlkeZuQfMaD/~/edit/~/changes/16/protocol-resources/risk-disclosure) in full before depositing.
